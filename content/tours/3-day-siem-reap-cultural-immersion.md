# 3-Day Siem Reap Cultural Immersion

This intensive three-day program offers students a deep dive into both ancient and contemporary Khmer culture. Perfect for groups with limited time seeking maximum educational impact.

## Day 1: Ancient Wonders

**Morning:** Sunrise at Angkor Wat followed by a guided exploration of the temple complex, focusing on Khmer architecture and Hindu-Buddhist iconography.

**Afternoon:** Visit to Angkor Thom and Bayon Temple, with discussion of the shift from Hinduism to Mahayana Buddhism under Jayavarman VII.

**Evening:** Traditional Khmer dinner with cultural performance.

## Day 2: Beyond the Main Temples

**Morning:** Visit to Banteay Srei to see exceptional stone carving, followed by Ta Prohm to discuss nature's reclamation of built heritage.

**Afternoon:** Traditional arts workshop at Artisans Angkor, where students learn stone carving, woodcarving, or silk painting from master artisans.

**Evening:** Discussion session on Cambodian history and contemporary challenges.

## Day 3: Living Culture

**Morning:** Visit to a floating village on Tonlé Sap Lake, learning about traditional fishing communities and ecosystem dynamics.

**Afternoon:** Khmer cooking class featuring market visit and preparation of traditional dishes.

**Evening:** Farewell dinner and reflection session.

## Learning Outcomes

- Understanding of Khmer Empire history and religious evolution
- Appreciation for traditional Khmer arts and craftsmanship
- Insight into contemporary rural Cambodian life
- Hands-on experience with Khmer culinary traditions
