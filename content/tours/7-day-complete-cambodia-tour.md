# 7-Day Complete Cambodia Tour

This comprehensive seven-day program offers students a complete understanding of Cambodia, from ancient glories to contemporary challenges. Perfect for educational groups seeking maximum exposure.

## Days 1-3: Siem Reap - Ancient Civilization

**Day 1:** Arrival and orientation. Sunrise at Angkor Wat. Introduction to Khmer Empire history.

**Day 2:** Angkor Thom, Bayon, and Ta Prohm. Discussion of religious evolution and architectural achievement.

**Day 3:** Banteay Srei and Kbal Spean. Traditional arts workshop. Evening discussion on conservation challenges.

## Days 4-5: Phnom Penh - Modern History

**Day 4:** Flight to Phnom Penh. Tuol Sleng and Choeung Ek. Evening debrief on genocide studies.

**Day 5:** Royal Palace, National Museum, and contemporary art spaces. Meeting with local students.

## Days 6-7: Battambang - Rural Cambodia

**Day 6:** Travel to Battambang by boat (seasonal) or road. Colonial architecture tour. Traditional bamboo train. Phare Ponleu Selpak circus performance.

**Day 7:** Community service project at local school. Final reflection and departure.

## Learning Outcomes

- Comprehensive understanding of Cambodian history across three periods
- Exposure to urban and rural Cambodia
- Meaningful engagement with contemporary Cambodian society
- Hands-on contribution to local community
